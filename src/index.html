About

Do you need more privacy and protect your messages whether they are about business, brilliant ideas or simply confidential?


SecureTalk keeps your conversations secure by encoding your messages, which only your contact and you will be able to decode using a key and share them directly through WhatsApp.


Are you ready to start?

How does it work?

  1. Enter a key (from 1 to 25) or enter the one your contact sent you in the box to the right.
  2. Enter your message if you want to encode or paste the encoded message of your contact.
  3. Press encode to hide your message or decode to display the content of your contact's message.
  4. If you have an encoded message, send it to your contact by pressing the WhatsApp icon on the right.
  5. If you clear the entered values press restart.
